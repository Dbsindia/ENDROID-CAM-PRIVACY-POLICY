Privacy Policy

Endroid Cam - Smart Camera Management App

Last updated: 27-02-2026

DBS SECURITY SYSTEMS PVT. LTD. (“we”, “us”, or “our”) operates the Endroid Cam mobile application (“App”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the App.

By using Endroid Cam, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

1Information We Collect

Endroid Cam collects only the minimum data required for app functionality and security.

Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Profile photo (if provided)

This information is collected when you create an account or log in to the App.

Device & Camera Information

  • Camera device identifiers (UID) for P2P connectivity
  • Local network information (for camera discovery on your Wi-Fi network)
  • Device model and OS version (for compatibility and troubleshooting)

2Permissions We Request

Endroid Cam requests only the permissions necessary for its core camera management functionality:

Permission Purpose
Camera & Microphone Two-way audio communication with cameras
Location Required by Android for Wi-Fi network scanning & camera discovery
Bluetooth Wi-Fi camera provisioning / setup via BLE
Storage Saving camera recordings and snapshots locally
Notifications Receiving push alerts from your cameras
Internet & Network P2P live streaming, cloud sync, and remote access

We do not use any of these permissions to track your location or collect data beyond what is needed for app functionality.

3How We Use Your Information

  • User authentication and account management
  • Secure peer-to-peer (P2P) connectivity to your cameras
  • Live video streaming and playback functionality
  • Camera discovery, setup, and configuration
  • Push notifications for camera alerts and events
  • App performance optimization and crash reporting
  • Customer support and service communication

We do not use your data for advertising or marketing purposes.

4Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Your data may be shared only:

  • When required by law or legal obligation
  • To protect the security, rights, or property of users or DBS SECURITY SYSTEMS PVT. LTD.

5Data Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data against:

  • Unauthorized access
  • Loss or misuse
  • Alteration or disclosure

Camera streams use encrypted peer-to-peer connections. Account credentials are securely stored on-device. However, no digital system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6Data Retention

Your personal data is retained only for as long as your account is active.

  • Locally stored recordings and snapshots remain on your device and are under your control.
  • If you delete your account, your personal data will be permanently deleted from our servers.

7Account Deletion

You can delete your Endroid Cam account at any time:

  • From within the App (Endroid Cam app → Profile → Delete Account), or
  • By contacting us through the official support channel

Once deleted:

  • Your account and personal data (name, email, phone number, profile photo) are permanently removed
  • All associated camera configurations on our servers are deleted

8Children’s Privacy

Endroid Cam is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

9Third-Party Services

Endroid Cam uses the following third-party services strictly for app functionality:

  • Firebase Authentication — for secure user sign-in
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging — for push notifications
  • Firebase Crashlytics — for crash reporting and stability

These services operate under their own privacy policies. We do not share personal data with third parties for advertising or analytics beyond what is listed above.

Any future integrations will comply with Google Play policies and applicable data protection laws.

10Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page, and the “Last updated” date will be revised accordingly. Continued use of the App after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
